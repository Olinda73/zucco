Welcome to

Buttermilk & Maple

BUTTERMILK AND MAPLE BRISTOL IS LOCATED ON THE FAMOUS WELSH BACK WATERFRONT, CLOSE TO THE HISTORICAL BRISTOL BRIDGE THAT GAVE LIFE TO THE CITY.

FOR CENTURIES THE BRIDGE WAS THE ONLY CROSSING OF THE AVON FOR MILES AROUND. AS BRISTOL SPREAD TO THE SOUTHERN BANK OF THE RIVER, THE BRIDGE JOINED THE TWO HALVES OF THE CITY. SO A GEORGIAN BRIDGE TOLL CAUSED OUTRAGE AND LED TO BRISTOL'S BLOODIEST RIOT.

RELAX HISTORY IS THE PAST!! BUTTERMILK AND MAPLE IS THE PERFECT PLACE TO MEET WITH COLLEAGUES, CATCH-UP WITH FRIENDS, SHARE PRECIOUS MOMENTS WITH FAMILY OR JUST TO BE ON YOUR OWN CHILLING OUT, WE HAVE FREE WIFI AND PLENTY OF POWER SOCKETS NEAR TABLES TO KEEP YOU CONNECTED ALL DAY.

WE SERVE GREAT FOOD FROM AN EARLY BUFFET BREAKFAST, TRENDY BRUNCH, LIGHT LUNCH… OR NOT, AFTERNOON TEA AND DINNER THAT YOU CAN ENJOY IN A VERY LAID BACK ATMOSPHERE. DON’T FORGET TO ORDER THE PANCAKES!

OUR TEAM WILL ALSO BE DELIGHTED TO GUIDE YOU DISCOVERING THE FLAVORS OF OUR BAR WITH A GREAT SELECTION OF WINES, CRAFT BEER, GINS, WHISKEYS AND OTHER SPIRITS THAT ARE ALSO PART OF INGREDIENTS USED TO CREATE FANTASTIC COCKTAILS.

WE ALSO HAVE MEETING ROOMS AND A MEZANINE TO HOST MEETINGS OR SPECIAL EVENTS.

JUST POP IN, CONTACT US … AND FOLLOW US ON SOCIAL MEDIA TO KEEP IN TOUCH AND SEE WHAT’S HAPP EVERY DAY!!!
